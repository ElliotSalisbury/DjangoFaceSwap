About Me

A PhD student interested in computer vision and machine learning

I am a PhD student at the University of Southampton.
I am interested in developing exciting new applications with computer vision.
My research has been focused on developing human-intelligent sensor by combining computer vision, AI, and real-time crowdsourcing. This sensor can locate and track, in real-time, any object described by natural language. For example, this can then be used to inform a robot where or what an object is, and enable them to autonomously carry out natural language instructions.
