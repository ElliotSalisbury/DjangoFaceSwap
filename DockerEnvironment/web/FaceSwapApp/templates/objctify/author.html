About Me

A PhD student interested in computer vision and machine learning

I am a PhD student at the University of Southampton.
I am interested in developing exciting new applications with computer vision.
My research has been focused on developing a system for real-time detection and tracking of targets described in natural language. It uses real-time crowdsourcing with computer vision and machine learning to create a human-intelligent sensor feed that works in new environments and on untrained targets. For example, this can then be used in disaster response scenarios, where search requirements are constantly changing, the environment is different every deployment, and with little ground truth data available. it can be used to inform a robotic agent where or what a search target is, and enable them to autonomously carry out natural language instructions.
